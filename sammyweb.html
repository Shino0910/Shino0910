Makeup by Sammy

Makeup artist and hair stylist

based in Vancouver, Canada and Taipei,Taiwan.

Inspired by colors, weather, nature and moments shared together, Sammy's creative looks for art will encourage you to create your existential love and curiosity.


My name is Sammy and I'm a Vancouver and Taipei fashion, high fashion and natural beauty makeup artist. More importantly, I am a down to earth person who wants a life story filled with creativity worth sharing.

I began my makeup journey 5 years ago, starting with international makeup program at Blanche MacDonald Centre in Vancouver.

My experience in both the fashion industry and marketing world, has taught me that I love working with people who are passionate and respect the creative process.
